About

About the Plant QR system and the administrator.

About the system

Plant QR is a campus plant and tree information system. You can browse trees added in the campus, see their locations on a map, and view details. The system is managed by the campus administrator.

About the administrator

The administrator manages user access, adds and removes trees, and maintains tree data and locations. For any questions, feedback, or to report a problem, use the Feedback, Report problems, or Contact pages.
