Getting Started

Welcome to Plant QR System

The Plant QR System allows you to browse and explore information about plants and trees in your campus. This guide will help you navigate and use all the features available to you.

Navigation

Sidebar Menu

The sidebar on the left contains all the main navigation options:

  • Dashboard - Your main overview page with statistics and quick actions
  • Plants - Browse all registered plants and trees
  • Feedback - Submit feedback about the system
  • Report problems - Report any issues you encounter
  • Contact - Contact information for support
  • About - Information about the system and administrators
  • Profile - Manage your account settings

Dashboard Overview

What You'll See

When you log in, you'll land on the Dashboard page which shows:

  • Statistics Cards - Overview of total plants and active plants
  • Quick Actions - Shortcuts to commonly used features
  • Support & Info - Links to help and support resources
  • Tree History - A list of all trees added to the campus
  • Tree Locations - GPS locations of trees with links to view them on Google Maps

Browsing Plants

How to Browse

  1. Click on "Plants" in the sidebar menu
  2. You'll see a grid of all available plants and trees
  3. Each plant card shows:
    • Plant name and scientific name
    • Location
    • Date added
  4. Click on "View Details" to see more information about a specific plant

Searching and Filtering

On the Plants page, you can:

  • Use the search box to find plants by name, location, or scientific name
  • Filter plants by status (Active/Inactive) using the dropdown menu

Viewing Plant Details

Plant Information

When you click on a plant to view details, you'll see:

  • Basic Information - Name, scientific name, location, and date added
  • QR Code - A QR code that can be scanned to access plant information
  • Additional Details - Description, care instructions, and other relevant information

Using QR Codes

Each plant has a unique QR code that can be:

  • Downloaded or printed for physical placement near the plant
  • Scanned with a mobile device to quickly access plant information
  • Shared via URL link

Tree Locations

Finding Trees on Campus

The Dashboard includes a section showing all tree locations:

  • View the list of all trees with their locations
  • Click "View on map" to open the tree location in Google Maps
  • Use GPS coordinates to navigate to specific trees

Profile Management

Accessing Your Profile

  1. Click on "Profile" in the sidebar menu
  2. View your account information and statistics
  3. Click "Edit Profile" to update your information (if available)

Getting Help

Support Options

If you need assistance, you can:

  • Submit Feedback - Share your thoughts and suggestions via the Feedback page
  • Report Problems - Let administrators know about any issues you encounter
  • Contact Support - Reach out directly through the Contact page
  • View About - Learn more about the system and administrators

Tips and Best Practices

  • Use the search function to quickly find specific plants
  • Bookmark frequently accessed plant pages
  • Check the Dashboard regularly for updates on new plants
  • Use the tree locations feature to plan your campus visits
  • Share QR codes with others who might be interested in the plants

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I add new plants?

Only administrators can add new plants to the system. If you'd like to suggest a plant to be added, please use the Feedback or Contact page.

How do I scan a QR code?

Use your mobile device's camera app or a QR code scanner app to scan the QR code displayed on the plant details page or on physical signs near plants.

Can I download QR codes?

Yes, QR codes can be downloaded or copied from the plant details page for printing or sharing.

How often is the information updated?

Plant information is updated by administrators. Check back regularly for new additions and updates.

Need More Help?

If you have questions that aren't answered in this guide, please don't hesitate to:

  • Contact support through the Contact page
  • Submit feedback via the Feedback page
  • Report any problems you encounter
